What is CPP Senior Project?


As one of the two polytechnic universities in the California, we value the hands-on learning experience. With the Senior Project, the students will have the opportunity to practice their understanding and demonstrate their ability to apply what they've learned in a team setting.


Why CIS Does a Rigorous Senior Project

According to Joel Spolsky in his Capstone projects and time management article, most Computer Science students do not have the opportunities to experience the real-world work environment where they have to collaborate with other colleagues and resolve client-facing issues. The knowledge they gain through the classroom is only the building block to prepare them for their career. Threfore, we want to prepare our students for the real-world enviroment and situations before they done with school and step into their carrers.
